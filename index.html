WattWorks: A-level circuit modeller

A circuit modelling tool built as part of my A-Level NEA, now continuing as a personal project.

About

WattWorks started as my A-Level Computer Science NEA project.

As someone who found the topic of electricity counterintuitive to learn, I wanted to build something to help demonstrate the topic in a visual way.

After finishing the coursework, I decided to keep developing it further for fun. This site is a way to share progress.
